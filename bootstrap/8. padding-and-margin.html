🧱 Bootstrap Spacing Utilities

1. .m-3 - Margin of 1rem (16px) on all sides.

2. .p-4 - Padding of 1.5rem (24px) on all sides.

3. .mt-5 - Margin-top of 3rem (48px).

4. .px-5 - Padding-left and right of 3rem (48px).

5. .mx-4 & .py-3 - Margin-x is 1.5rem, Padding-y is 1rem.

6. .m-0 - No margin at all.

7. .m-n3 - Negative margin (pulls element closer).

8. .p-2 (small screens), .p-md-5 (medium+)
