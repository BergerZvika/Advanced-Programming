Headings & Paragraphs

Heading Levels

HTML has six heading levels. Use them in document order — don't skip levels.

h1 — Page Title (one per page)

h2 — Section

h3 — Sub-section

h4 — Sub-sub-section

h5 — Rarely used
h6 — Rarely used

Paragraphs

The <p> tag wraps a block of text. Browsers add top and bottom margin automatically.
