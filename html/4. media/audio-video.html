Audio & Video

  <source src="audio.mp3" type="audio/mpeg">
  <source src="audio.ogg" type="audio/ogg">
  Your browser doesn't support the audio element.
</audio>

Audio Attributes

AttributeEffect
controlsShow browser's play/pause/volume bar
autoplayPlay immediately (blocked by browsers without muted)
mutedStart muted (allows autoplay to work)
loopReplay from the start when finished
preloadnone / metadata / auto — hint to browser how much to preload

<video>

<video controls width="640" poster="thumbnail.jpg">
  <source src="video.mp4" type="video/mp4">
  <source src="video.webm" type="video/webm">
  <track kind="subtitles" src="captions.vtt" srclang="en" label="English">
  Your browser doesn't support the video element.
</video>

Video-specific Attributes

AttributeEffect
posterImage shown before the video plays (thumbnail)
width / heightDimensions in pixels
playsinlinePlay inline on iOS instead of fullscreen
<track>Subtitle/caption file in WebVTT format — required for accessibility

Embedding YouTube / Vimeo

External videos are embedded with <iframe>, not <video>:

<iframe
  width="560" height="315"
  src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/VIDEO_ID"
  title="Video title"
  allowfullscreen>
</iframe>
