colspan & rowspan

colspan — span across columns

colspan="N" makes a cell occupy N columns horizontally.

Q1 Results (colspan="3")
January February March
$2,400 $1,900 $2,800

Code: <th colspan="3">Q1 Results</th> in the first row, then three separate <th> cells in the second row.

rowspan — span across rows

rowspan="N" makes a cell occupy N rows vertically. The spanned rows omit that cell.
