Form Structure

The <form> Element

AttributeEffect
actionURL where form data is sent (omit for JS-handled forms)
methodget (data in URL) or post (data in request body)
enctypemultipart/form-data required for file uploads
novalidateSkip browser built-in validation (handle in JS instead)
autocompleteon or off for the whole form

label Association

Always link labels to inputs — clicking the label focuses the input, and screen readers announce both together.

<!-- Method 1: for/id pairing (most common) -->
<label for="username">Username</label>
<input type="text" id="username" name="username">

<!-- Method 2: wrapping (implicit) -->
<label>
  Username
  <input type="text" name="username">
</label>

select & textarea

<!-- Dropdown -->
<select name="country">
  <option value="">-- choose --</option>
  <option value="de">Germany</option>
  <option value="us" selected>United States</option>
  <optgroup label="Asia">
    <option value="jp">Japan</option>
    <option value="kr">Korea</option>
  </optgroup>
</select>

<!-- Multi-line text -->
<textarea name="bio" rows="4" cols="40"
          maxlength="500" placeholder="Tell us about yourself…"></textarea>
